UX Strategy

A good designer must rely on experience, on precise, logic thinking; and on pedantic exactness. No magic will do.

Niklaus Wirth

UX Strategy is primarily focused on knowing what delights and engages your users, and molding theproduct (or design) accordingly. Since the experience extends well beyond the computer screen, we develop a plan that incorporates a launch marketing strategy in order to set your product up for success.

With UX Strategy, we can determine:

  • Clear goals and objectives
  • Who are we building this product for?
  • Does this impact the technology we use?
  • What steps we need to take to promote your product?
  • Examine your competition and surpass them?

Services include:

  • Visioning sessions and workshops
  • Persona creation workshops
  • Product voice and tone sessions
  • Heuristic evalutation of current application

User Research

Know thy users. Your designs are only as good as your research.

People are complex. This means without a profound understanding of your users’ motivations and needs, developing an effective design is tricky. We aim to save time and money by resolving any issues earlier, rather than later.

We create and refine scripts and facilitate to get the types of insights we hope to find, and use that datato measure our results after development.

UX Research can answer:

  • What is the problem in the space? Is building this a good idea?
  • Does my product make sence to the people who need it the most?
  • What are going to be the biggest challenges?
  • What makes this product more awesome than the competition?
  • What story is our data currently telling?

Services include:

  • Usability testing
  • User Interviews
  • Contextual Interviews
  • Task Analysis
  • Field Research

UI Design

Memorable design is simple. That's why it's so complex.

The interface is more than just making an app look pretty. With online products, the user interface is a significant part of the experience and getting this right is important. The goal is to create an interface that is clear, give users autonomy, rich interaction and treats them like friends.

Great UI design can:

  • Build user trust
  • Create a great first impression
  • Create user delight
  • Communicate important information
  • Improve overall user satisfaction

Services include:

  • User Interface Design
  • Art Direction

Wireframing & Prototyping

Test fast. Adjust fast.

Have a wonderful idea you want to test before investing more resources for further development? Prototyping is the perfect solution. We examine and test the design with your target users to assess what works and what doesn’t, then we can start building.

Wireframing and prototyping can help:

  • Validate Your Business
  • Uncover Usability Issues
  • Help determine feature set
  • Provide Better Functional Specs
  • Secure Funding

Services include:

  • Wireframing of user flows, functions and architecture
  • Creating clickable and interactive protoypes
